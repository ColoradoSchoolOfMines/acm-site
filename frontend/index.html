Mines ACM Student Chapter

Mines ACM is the largest Computer Science club at Mines and strives to provide meaningful career opportunities and CS-related experiences to students.

We host weekly tech talks from recognized industry professionals, project meetings with diverse and open source projects, workshops teaching industry skills, and more.

Mines ACM is an official local chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery and is an official club of CS@Mines.

To join Mines ACM, simply show up to our meetings every Tuesday at 6:00pm. Join our Discord and mailing list for weekly meeting details & interaction with the CS@Mines community.
